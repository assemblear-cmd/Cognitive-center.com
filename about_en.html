About Us

Mission

Our mission is the safe singularity of artificial and natural intelligence.

Tasks

Our tasks on the path to achieving a safe singularity:

  1. Systematic development and integration of cognitive education
  2. Research and prevention of "Cognitive Immunity"
  3. Support for AI education
  4. Ensuring the accessibility of AI tools for a wide range of people
  5. Promoting the use of neurointerfaces for a wide range of people

Who We Are

We are a group of researchers and practitioners in the fields of AI, bioengineering, psychiatry, and education, united around the mission and to solve the tasks of the Center.
