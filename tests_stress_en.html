Psychological Screening Tests

All tests are based on clinically approved screening methodologies.

Columbia-Suicide Severity Rating Scale (C-SSRS)

Screening and assessment of suicide risk severity (6 questions).

Trauma Screening Questionnaire (TSQ)

Screening for post-traumatic stress disorder (10 questions).

Hospital Anxiety and Depression Scale (HADS)

Assessment of anxiety and depression levels (14 questions).

Questionnaire for Assessing the Mental State of a Child (2-6 years)

For parents: assessment of signs of a crisis state in preschoolers (14 items).

Child Behavior Observation Map (7-17 years)

For parents: identification of signs of maladaptive behavior in schoolchildren (9 items).
